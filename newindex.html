HackDecatur

Let's hack a city

The Intro

HackDecatur is all about helping the City of Decatur through software. For one weekend, we'll all come together to write code that benefits the entire city of Decatur, not just one business or cause.

The Issue

Decatur has a thriving business scene, with new restaurants, stores, and clubs opening every other week. All of these businesses have problems they need solved. That's where you come in.

In the modern age, software can solve just about any problem that can be solved. Unfortunately, the true power of code isn't realized by very many people.

The Goal

At HackDecatur, we want to draw together talented technologists, designers, business owners, and idea people to make Decatur's organizations more efficient. People from all backgrounds are welcome.
