The Intro

HackDecatur is all about helping the City of Decatur through software. For one weekend, we'll all come together to write code that benefits the entire city of Decatur, not just one business or cause.

Decatur has a thriving business scene, with new restaurants, stores, and venues opening every other week. All of these businesses have problems they need solved. That's where you come in.

The Problem & The Solution

Outside of the technology industry, code literacy is scarce in many businesses. Due to the fact that software is used less commonly to solve problems, owners of large and small businesses alike face trivial issues that could be solved by simple web apps, mobile interfaces, or even just simple databases.

At HackDecatur, we want to draw together talented technologists, designers, business owners, and idea people to tackle the task of making Decatur's organizations more efficient. Local businesses around the area that have chosen to participate in HackDecatur have presented problems for the attendees to solve using code. For each problem solved, a local business becomes more technologically oriented, and an attendee claims a bounty put forth by said local business. It's a win-win. =======
