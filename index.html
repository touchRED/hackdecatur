The Intro

HackDecatur is all about helping the City of Decatur through software. For one weekend, we'll all come together to write code that benefits the entire city of Decatur, not just one business or cause.

The Issue

Decatur has a thriving business scene, with new restaurants, stores, and venues opening every other week. All of these businesses have problems they need solved. That's where you come in.

The Solution

In the modern age, software can solve just about any problem that can be solved. Unfortunately, the true power of code isn't realized by very many people. Outside of the technology industry, code literacy is scarce in many businesses. Due to the fact that software is used less commonly to solve problems, owners of large and small businesses alike face trivial issues that could be solved by simple web apps, mobile interfaces, or even just simple databases.

At HackDecatur, we want to draw together talented technologists, designers, business owners, and idea people to tackle the task of making Decatur's organizations more efficient. People from all backgrounds are welcome to attend. As long as you bring an idea, we're happy to have you. But who are "we"?

The People

HackDecatur has been organized by a small group of interesting people that truly want to better the Decatur community and business scene. They come from a variety of backgrounds.
